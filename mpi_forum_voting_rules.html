Voting Rules

  • One vote per eligible voting organization
  • An organization is eligible to vote if a member of the organization has been at two of the last three fact-to-face meetings
  • Votes are taken twice, as separate meetings.  Votes are preceded by a reading at an earlier meeting, to familiarize everyone with the issues.
  • Measures pass on a simple majority.
  • Only items consistent with the charter will be considered.
